We ship from the UK. Orders are processed within 1-3 working days.

Free UK shipping on orders over £50.

We're not Amazon. We don't have drones or same-day delivery. But we do have tracking numbers and a genuine desire to get your stuff to you in one piece.

RETURN POLICY.

Faulty or damaged items: If your order arrives damaged or there's a fault with the product, submit a return request within 14 days with photos. We'll sort it — either a replacement or full refund.

Wrong size: If the item is unworn with tags still on, we'll do an exchange. You cover the return postage. Check the size guide before ordering to avoid this.

Changed your mind: We don't offer refunds for items you simply don't want anymore. Think of it as a life lesson in commitment.

Mystery Boxes: Non-refundable. That's literally the point of a mystery box.
