RETURNS & SHIPPING.

The boring but necessary stuff. We'll keep it short.

SHIPPING.

We ship from the UK. Orders are processed within 1-3 working days.

Destination Method Estimated Time Cost
UK Royal Mail Tracked 3-5 working days £3.99
Europe Royal Mail International 5-10 working days £7.99
Rest of World Royal Mail International 7-14 working days £9.99

Free UK shipping on orders over £50.

We're not Amazon. We don't have drones or same-day delivery. But we do have tracking numbers and a genuine desire to get your stuff to you in one piece.

RETURNS.

Faulty or damaged items: If your order arrives damaged or there's a fault with the product, email us within 14 days at hello@dirtboxclub.co.uk with photos. We'll sort it — either a replacement or full refund.

Wrong size: We get it, sizing is annoying. If the item is unworn with tags still on, we'll do an exchange. You cover the return postage. Check the size guide before ordering to avoid this.

Changed your mind: That's a you problem. We don't do refunds because you decided you don't like it after buying it. Think of it as a life lesson in commitment.

Mystery Boxes: Non-refundable. That's literally the point of a mystery box.

CANCELLATIONS.

If you need to cancel an order, email us as soon as possible. If we haven't shipped it yet, we'll cancel it. If it's already on its way, see the returns section above.
