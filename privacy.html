PRIVACY POLICY.

The legal bit. We've tried to make it less painful than usual.

WHO WE ARE.

We're Dirt Box Club, a UK-based clothing and merchandise brand. Our website is dirtboxclub.co.uk. When we say "we," "us," or "our," we mean Dirt Box Club.

WHAT WE COLLECT.

When you sign up as a member: Email address, first name (optional), t-shirt size (optional), birthday month (optional), how you found us, and your quiz results.

When you place an order: Name, email, delivery address, and payment information. Payment is processed securely through our payment provider — we don't store your card details.

When you browse the site: Basic analytics data (pages visited, time on site) via cookies.

WHY WE COLLECT IT.

To send you the stuff you ordered. To email you about new drops and member stuff (only if you opted in). To send you a birthday discount. To understand where our customers come from so we can find more of you. To plan stock based on size data.

We don't collect data for the sake of it. We're not Facebook.

WHO WE SHARE IT WITH.

Nobody. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with third parties for marketing. The only people who see your info are us and our payment/shipping providers (who need it to actually deliver your order).

YOUR RIGHTS.

You can ask us to delete your data at any time. You can unsubscribe from emails at any time. You can ask to see what data we hold on you. Just email hello@dirtboxclub.co.uk and we'll sort it.

COOKIES.

We use cookies to keep the site working and to understand how people use it. Nothing sinister. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you want, but some things might not work properly.

CONTACT.

Questions about your data? Email hello@dirtboxclub.co.uk.
