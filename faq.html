FAQ

QUESTIONS WE'RE TIRED OF ANSWERING.

BUT HERE WE ARE.

Do you do refunds?

If the item's faulty, yeah. If you just changed your mind, that's a you problem. We're not your therapist.

How long does delivery take?

Depends where you live and how much the universe likes you that week. We're not Amazon and we're not sorry about it. UK orders usually 3-5 working days.

Can I suggest a design?

You can. Whether we listen is a different conversation entirely. But go on then – we're not completely heartless.

Is this brand offensive?

Only if you let it be. If a t-shirt ruins your day, we've got bigger questions for you.

Someone asks what Dirt Box means. What do I say?

A. You wouldn't get it. B. Sorry I don't speak English. C. It means you're a nob. Dealer's choice.

Do you ship internationally?

We do. The world deserves better merch. Takes a bit longer because geography is a thing that exists.
